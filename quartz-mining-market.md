<p><strong>The Quartz Mining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary Quartz (SiO2 Below 95%)</li><li>Refined Quartz (SiO2 95%-99%)</li><li>High-Purity Quartz (SiO2 Above 99%)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quartz mining market is categorized based on the purity of silica content. Ordinary quartz contains SiO2 below 95% and is commonly used in basic industrial applications. Refined quartz, with SiO2 levels ranging from 95% to 99%, serves in more specialized sectors such as electronics and ceramics. High-purity quartz, exceeding 99% SiO2, is crucial for high-tech industries, including semiconductors and solar cells, where superior purity is essential for optimal performance. Each type serves distinct applications based on purity requirements.</p></p>

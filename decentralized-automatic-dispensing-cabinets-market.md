<p><p>The Decentralized Automatic Dispensing Cabinets (ADCs) market is characterized by two main types: stationary and mobile dispensing cabinets. Stationary dispensing cabinets are fixed installations, typically located in pharmacies or healthcare facilities, designed to securely store and dispense medications efficiently while maintaining inventory control. Both types enhance medication management and safety in decentralized healthcare environments.</p></p>

<p><p>The rotation torque sensors market is segmented into dynamic and static types. Dynamic torque sensors measure torque in real time during rotating operations, making them ideal for applications like motors and gear systems where continuous monitoring is crucial. Conversely, static torque sensors are used to measure torque without motion, typically during assembly or testing processes. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, where precision in torque measurement ensures optimal performance and safety.</p></p>

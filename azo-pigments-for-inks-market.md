<p><p>Azo pigments for inks are classified into several types based on their chemical structure. Monoazo pigments contain a single azo group and offer bright, vivid colors, making them suitable for various printing applications. Disazo pigments, with two azo groups, provide excellent lightfastness and stability, ideal for high-quality inks. Azo metal complex pigments incorporate metal ions to enhance color properties and durability. The "Others" category includes unique azo variations or blends that cater to specific requirements in the ink industry.</p></p>

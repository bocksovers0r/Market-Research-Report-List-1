<p><strong>The Medical Sterilization Trolley Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed-structure</li><li>Opened-structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical sterilization trolley market comprises two main types: closed-structure and open-structure trolleys. Closed-structure trolleys feature enclosed compartments that protect sterilized instruments from contaminants and maintain sterility during transport. These trolleys are ideal for environments requiring strict hygiene protocols. In contrast, open-structure trolleys provide easier access to instruments and facilitate quick transportation, but they may not offer the same level of protection against contamination. Both types cater to different needs within healthcare facilities, enhancing efficiency in sterilization processes.</p></p>

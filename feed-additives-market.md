<p><p>The feed additives market can be segmented into two main types: dry and liquid. Conversely, liquid feed additives are typically used for specific nutritional purposes, often enhancing palatability or improving digestibility. Both forms serve essential roles in livestock production, optimizing growth, boosting immune responses, and improving overall animal performance.</p></p>

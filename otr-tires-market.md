<p><p>The OTR (Off-The-Road) tire market includes three main types: radial tires, bias tires, and solid tires. Radial tires feature layers of fabric arranged perpendicular to the tread, providing better flexibility and longevity, ideal for rough terrains. Bias tires have layers arranged at a diagonal, offering enhanced stability and load-carrying capacity, commonly used in mining. Solid tires are made from resilient materials, eliminating the risk of punctures, making them suitable for heavy industrial applications. Each type serves specific operational needs in challenging environments.</p></p>

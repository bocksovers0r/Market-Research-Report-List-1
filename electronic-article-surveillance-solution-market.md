<p><ul><li>RF Electronic Article Surveillance Solution</li><li>AM Electronic Article Surveillance Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) market primarily consists of two main types: Radio Frequency (RF) and Acousto-Magnetic (AM) electronic article surveillance solutions. RF systems utilize radio waves to detect security tags and are often favored for their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. In contrast, AM systems employ magnetic fields to detect tags, offering enhanced security against theft and improved detection accuracy, especially in challenging environments. Both types are widely used in retail to prevent loss and ensure inventory security.</p></p>

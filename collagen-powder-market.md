<p><p>The collagen powder market is primarily categorized into three types: gelatin, hydrolyzed collagen, and others. Gelatin, derived from boiling animal connective tissues, maintains its protein structure but is less soluble in cold water. Hydrolyzed collagen, or collagen peptides, is broken down for easier absorption and mixes well in cold beverages. The "others" category encompasses various forms and sources, including plant-based alternatives and specialty formulations, which cater to diverse consumer preferences for health, beauty, and dietary needs.</p></p>

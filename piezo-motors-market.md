<p><p>The piezo motors market is primarily segmented into AC and DC piezo motors, each serving distinct applications. AC piezo motors utilize alternating current to drive oscillations, offering precise positioning and high-speed operations, making them ideal for applications in optics and precision machining. In contrast, DC piezo motors operate on direct current, providing excellent torque and control at low speeds, often used in medical devices and robotics. Both types leverage piezoelectric materials to achieve efficient motion control and positioning.</p></p>

<p><p>The sponge rubber market is categorized into three main types: closed-cell, open-cell, and other types. Closed-cell rubber features a dense structure, providing excellent insulation and water resistance, making it ideal for applications like seals and gaskets. Open-cell rubber, on the other hand, has interconnected cells, allowing for breathability and cushioning, commonly used in mattresses and automotive interiors. The "other" category encompasses various specialty rubbers with unique properties for specific applications, such as anti-vibration and soundproofing solutions.</p></p>

<p><p>The semiconductor assembly equipment market encompasses various types of machinery essential for packaging and assembling semiconductor devices. Die bonders precisely attach semiconductor die to substrates, ensuring accurate placement. Wire bonders create electrical connections between the die and leads using fine wires. Packaging equipment facilitates the encasement of chips, protecting them from environmental factors. Additionally, "others" category includes various ancillary tools and equipment, such as molders and testers, which support the assembly process, enhancing efficiency and product reliability.</p></p>

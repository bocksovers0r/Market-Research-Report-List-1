<p><ul><li>Single Element</li><li>Dual Element</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PIR-based motion detector market can be categorized into three main types: Single Element, Dual Element, and Others. Single Element detectors utilize a single pyro-electric sensor to detect motion and are cost-effective, making them suitable for basic applications. Dual Element detectors feature two sensors that enhance sensitivity and reduce false alarms by comparing signals from both elements. The "Others" category encompasses advanced sensors, such as multi-element detectors or those combined with other technologies, offering higher accuracy and broader applications in security and automation.</p></p>

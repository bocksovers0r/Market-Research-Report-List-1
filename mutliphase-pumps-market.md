<p><ul><li>Twin-screw pump (TSP)</li><li>Helicoaxial pump (HAP)</li><li>Progressing-cavity pump (PCP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multiphase pumps market comprises several key types, including twin-screw pumps (TSP), helicoaxial pumps (HAP), and progressing-cavity pumps (PCP). TSPs utilize two intermeshing screws to handle various fluid phases efficiently. HAPs employ a helical design to promote mixing and flow in challenging conditions, making them ideal for high-viscosity applications. PCPs feature a helical rotor inside a cavity, enabling effective pumping of low-viscosity and multiphase fluids. Each type serves distinct industrial needs in oil and gas production and other sectors.</p></p>

<p><p>The non-biodegradable skin filler market primarily consists of three types: Calcium Hydroxylapatite, PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate), and other materials. PMMA is a synthetic compound that creates a permanent filler effect, often used for deeper wrinkles. Other materials may include various polymer blends or biocompatible substances, providing diverse options for aesthetic treatments, each with distinct characteristics and applications in cosmetic procedures.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Disposable Apheresis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugation</li><li>Membrane Separation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable apheresis market is primarily categorized into two types: centrifugation and membrane separation. </p><p>Centrifugation uses centrifugal force to separate blood components based on density, efficiently isolating plasma or specific cells. This method is widely utilized for therapeutic and diagnostic purposes. </p><p>Membrane separation, on the other hand, employs selective permeability to differentiate blood components, relying on size or charge. This technique is increasingly favored for its lower operational costs and minimal mechanical complexity, enhancing patient safety.</p></p>

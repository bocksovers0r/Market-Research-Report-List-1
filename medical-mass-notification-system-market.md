<p><p>The Medical Mass Notification System market encompasses two primary types: In-building Mass Notification System and Outdoor Mass Notification System. In-building systems are designed to deliver urgent alerts within a facility, ensuring timely communication to staff and patients during emergencies like fires or medical crises. Conversely, Outdoor Mass Notification Systems focus on alerting people in open areas, utilizing sirens, loudspeakers, or mobile alerts to broadcast information about threats or emergencies to the surrounding community effectively. Both systems aim to enhance safety and responsiveness in medical settings.</p></p>

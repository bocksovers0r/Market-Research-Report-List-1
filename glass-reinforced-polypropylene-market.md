<p><p>The Glass-Reinforced Polypropylene market is divided into two main types: Long Glass Fiber Reinforced Polypropylene (LGFPP) and Short Glass Fiber Reinforced Polypropylene (SGFPP). LGFPP offers enhanced mechanical properties and durability, making it ideal for applications requiring high strength and rigidity, such as automotive components. In contrast, SGFPP is more flexible and easier to process, suitable for applications where weight reduction and cost efficiency are prioritized. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, consumer goods, and construction.</p></p>

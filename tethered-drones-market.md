<p><p>The tethered drones market consists of three main types: Ground Fixed Type, Vehicle Mounted Mobile Type, and Shipborne Mobile Type. Ground Fixed Type drones are stationary systems anchored to a specific location, ideal for surveillance and monitoring. Vehicle Mounted Mobile Type drones are integrated into ground vehicles, allowing for mobility while maintaining tethered connectivity, enhancing operational flexibility. Shipborne Mobile Type drones are deployed from vessels, providing aerial support for maritime applications, ensuring persistent surveillance and communication over water bodies.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Vinyl Composition Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vinyl Composition Flooring (VCF) is categorized into two main market types: single-layer and multilayer. Single-layer VCF consists of a single sheet of vinyl, making it lighter and easier to install, but generally less durable. In contrast, multilayer VCF features multiple layers, providing enhanced durability, better insulation, and improved acoustic properties. This multilayer construction often includes a backing layer for added comfort and stability, appealing to both residential and commercial applications seeking longevity and performance in flooring solutions.</p></p>

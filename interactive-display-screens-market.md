<p><p>The interactive display screens market primarily includes LCD and LED technologies. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) screens utilize liquid crystals and backlighting to create sharp images, making them suitable for educational and business environments. They are energy-efficient and offer good color accuracy. LED (Light Emitting Diode) screens, on the other hand, enhance image quality with greater brightness and contrast, often being more versatile for various lighting conditions. Both types cater to diverse applications, enhancing interactivity in presentations and collaborative workspaces.</p></p>

Hooks anchor the rods to the vertebrae, ensuring alignment. Plates offer fixation points for stabilization across spinal segments. Cages are inserted to maintain disc height and promote fusion. Pedicle screws secure rods to the vertebrae, enhancing the overall construct's integrity. Together, these elements facilitate effective spinal deformity correction procedures.</p></p>

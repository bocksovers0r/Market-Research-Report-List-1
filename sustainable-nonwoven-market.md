<p><ul><li>Drylaid Nonwovens</li><li>Wetlaid Nonwovens</li><li>Spunbond Nonwovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sustainable nonwoven market comprises various manufacturing types, including drylaid, wetlaid, and spunbond nonwovens. Drylaid nonwovens involve the consolidation of fibers using a dry process, ideal for products requiring high strength. Wetlaid nonwovens use a water suspension to form sheets, suitable for applications demanding uniformity and softness. Spunbond nonwovens are produced by melting polymer fibers, offering durability and lightweight properties, commonly used in hygiene and medical products. Each type contributes uniquely to sustainability in various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>The road transport services market consists of two primary types: full loads and part loads. Full load services involve transporting an entire truck's capacity dedicated to a single shipment, often preferred for cost efficiency and reduced handling. In contrast, part load services consolidate various smaller shipments from multiple customers within a single vehicle, making it ideal for those who do not require a full truckload. Both options provide flexibility and cater to different shipping needs in the logistics sector.</p></p>

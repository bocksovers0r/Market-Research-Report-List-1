<p><p>Rail turnout markets are categorized into various types based on their design and functionality. A single turnout allows a train to switch tracks at a single junction. Equilateral turnouts enable trains to switch between two tracks at equal angles, providing more flexibility. Three-way turnouts facilitate transitions to three different tracks from a single point, optimizing space in rail yards. Other types may include duple turnouts and special configurations designed for specific railway needs, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>

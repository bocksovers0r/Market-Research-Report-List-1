<p><p>The UV light disinfection market comprises several types, including Ordinary Cathode Low Pressure Mercury UV Disinfection, High-Intensity Ultraviolet Disinfection, and Ozone UV Disinfection. Ordinary Cathode Low Pressure Mercury systems utilize low-pressure mercury lamps for effective microbial reduction. High-Intensity Ultraviolet Disinfection employs powerful UV sources to rapidly eliminate pathogens. Ozone UV Disinfection combines ozone generation with UV light, enhancing disinfectant efficacy. Together, these technologies offer versatile solutions for water treatment, air purification, and surface sanitation across various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>The Siallon Bonded Silicon Carbide Bricks market encompasses three primary types based on production methods. </p><p>1. **Reaction Sintering** involves using silicon and carbon in the presence of nitrogen, creating durable and thermally stable bricks.</p><p>2. **Carbothermally Reduced Silica** utilizes the reduction of silica with carbon to produce silicon carbide, resulting in high-strength materials.</p><p>3. **Thermal Decomposition of Si(NH)2** employs the breakdown of silicon nitride to form silicon carbide, enhancing refractory properties. Each method offers unique advantages for specific industrial applications.</p></p>

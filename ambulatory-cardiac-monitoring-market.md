<p><ul><li>Holter Monitor</li><li>Event Monitors</li><li>Mobile Cardiac Telemetry</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ambulatory cardiac monitoring encompasses various types of devices used to assess heart activity outside of a clinical setting. Holter monitors continuously record heart rhythms for 24-48 hours, providing comprehensive data for diagnosing arrhythmias. Event monitors are used intermittently by patients to capture specific cardiac events when symptoms occur. Mobile cardiac telemetry offers real-time monitoring and wireless transmission of data for instant analysis. Additionally, other market devices include implantable monitors and wearable technologies, enhancing patient convenience and continuous monitoring capabilities.</p></p>

<p><p>The pet tracker market comprises three main types: GPS pet tracking systems, WiFi pet tracking systems, and radio pet tracking systems. GPS trackers utilize satellite technology for global location tracking, offering real-time updates and extensive coverage. WiFi trackers rely on local wireless networks, providing effective tracking in urban areas where WiFi is available. Radio pet tracking systems use radio frequencies for tracking over shorter distances, making them suitable for rural settings. Each type serves different needs based on location and technology availability.</p></p>

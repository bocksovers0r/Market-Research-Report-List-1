<p><strong>The 2-Axle Trailer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi Trailer</li><li>Full Trailer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 2-axle trailer market primarily consists of semi trailers and full trailers. Semi trailers are designed to be towed by a tractor unit, with part of the trailer's weight supported by the truck's chassis, allowing for higher payloads and improved maneuverability. In contrast, full trailers have front and rear axles, requiring a dedicated towing vehicle for support, which offers stability but limits flexibility in terms of maneuvering. Both types cater to different commercial transportation needs, influencing their market demand.</p></p>

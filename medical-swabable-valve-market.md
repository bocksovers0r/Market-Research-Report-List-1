<p><p>The medical swabable valve market is categorized into two main types: female thread and male thread valves. Female thread valves feature internal threading, allowing for secure connections with external components, making them ideal for applications requiring reliable sealing. Male thread valves, on the other hand, possess external threading that enables easy attachment to compatible fittings. Both types play a crucial role in medical devices, facilitating fluid control and maintaining sterility during procedures, thus ensuring optimal performance and patient safety.</p></p>

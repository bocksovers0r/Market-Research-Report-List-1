<p><p>The brain mapping instruments market includes various technologies used to visualize and analyze brain activity. Computed Tomography (CT) provides detailed images of brain structures through X-ray images, while Positron Emission Tomography (PET) measures metabolic processes by detecting radioactive tracers. Electroencephalography (EEG) records electrical activity via scalp electrodes, and Magnetoencephalography (MEG) measures magnetic fields produced by neuronal activity. Other market instruments may include functional MRI and near-infrared spectroscopy, contributing to a comprehensive understanding of brain function and disorders.</p></p>

<p><p>The PPS filaments market is primarily divided into two types: multifilament and monofilament. Multifilament consists of numerous thin fibers twisted together, offering flexibility and softness, making it suitable for applications like textiles and ropes. In contrast, monofilament is a single, thicker fiber, known for its strength and durability, commonly used in fishing lines, medical sutures, and industrial applications. Both types serve distinct purposes based on their structural properties, addressing the diverse needs of various industries.</p></p>
